About Us

Armax is a biomedical device for a company called Medik based in NYU Tandon in Brooklyn, New York. We specialize in interactive physical therapy exercises for children's arms connected to an engaging game. Our mission is to enhance children's physical therapy experience by making it fun and accessible, particularly for low-income families and free clinics. We pride ourselves on our high level of service and our commitment to affordability, without sacrificing quality. Explore the rest of our site to learn more about what we have to offer, and get in touch with any questions. Armax is here for you.
